Digital Trails

Ready to hike? Play Now!

This digitalized version of Trails is based on keymaster games' masterpiece: https://keymastergames.com/products/trails

TRAILS rules and components

5 trail sites -1 start (trail head) -1 end (trail end) 1 dice with 6 sides showing wildlife 1 bear token 2-4 hikers 1 sun token 20 Badge cards 25 Photo cards

Resources:

15 acorns 15 leafs 15 rocks 2 Canteens

CONCEPT

-Hikers (players) hike back and forth across trail sites (that make up the board) -Each turn, you gather resources and/or take photos -Random possibility of collecting bonuses from encountering wildlife -At space 1 or space 5 (aka trail head or trail end) you may trade in resources in exchange for badges -Each time a player visits the trail end, the sun sets further (aka moves towards trail end by 1 space) -Each time sun moves, resoucres/photo cards gained at trail sites increase by 1 -Gameplay ends when Sun reaches trail end -You win by collecting the most photos and badges worth the most points

EACH PLAYER

-each player is represented by a hiker. -starts out with 1 acorn, 1 rock, 1 stone, and a canteen
